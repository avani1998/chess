Problem Statement

Intro text goes here

Visualize:

Visualize player performance over time: By plotting a player's Elo rating over time, we can track their performance and see how it has changed over time. This can help identify trends and patterns in a player's performance and help predict future performance.

Compare:

Compare player performance: By creating a scatter plot of two players' Elo ratings over time, we can compare their performance and see how they stack up against each other. This can be useful for identifying strengths and weaknesses of each player and predicting the outcome of future matches.

Predict:

Employing predictive analytics in chess involves using statistical modeling and machine learning algorithms to analyze historical chess data and forecast future performance of a player.
